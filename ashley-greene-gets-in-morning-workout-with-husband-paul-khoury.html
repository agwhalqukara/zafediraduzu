Ashley Greene Gets in Morning Workout with Husband Paul Khoury

Ashley Greene is kicking off her weekend with a workout!

The 34-year-old actress, best known for playing Alice Cullen in the Twilight movies, was all smiles as she and husband Paul Khoury left a gym after a morning workout on Saturday (January 29) in Studio City, Calif.
