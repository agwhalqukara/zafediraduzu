The singer is not even considering being anyone’s side chick which is why, Bebe Rexha did not hesitate to slam a certain unnamed football player that has been texting her about getting together despite being married! And that’s not all! She then proceeded to share a text conversation screenshot in which the supposed footballer asks when they should meet in person.
