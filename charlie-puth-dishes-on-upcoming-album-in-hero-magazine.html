Charlie Puth Dishes On Upcoming Album In Hero Magazine

Charlie Puth is the face of the latest edition of ‘Hero’ magazine and not only does he look incredible, he is also dishing on writing, recording, as well as his upcoming sophomore album.

The magazine, out tomorrow, features Charlie showing off his playful side in the photo shoot – even taking his shirt off for some of the photos (and we’re definitely not complaining).

His new album, Voice Memos, is due out sometime later this year and we can’t wait. He recently posted on Instagram “Stacking vocals” along with a brief preview of new music. Have we already mentioned how excited we are?
