Chloe Bennet Biography

Chloe Bennet is best known as Actress, Singer who has an estimated Net Worth of $4 Million. American actress best known for playing Skye on Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. She has also hosted the TeenNick show The Nightlife. In 2017, she was cast in MGM's musical remake of Valley Girl alongside Logan Paul.   She was born on April 18,1992, Chicago, IL. She appeared in the music video “Tonight” by the Korean band Big Bang.  Before she pursued an acting career, she lived in China and made her name as a pop singer.  She had a supporting role on the television series Nashville, starring Connie Britton.  She grew up in Chicago, Illinois before moving to Beijing, China. She has seven brothers. She dated Logan Paul in 2018. She also dated Austin Nichols from 2013 to 2017.  Her zodiac sign is Aries
