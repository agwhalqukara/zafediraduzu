Gwen Stefani: Husband Gavin Rossdale &quot;Likes Me More&quot; With Makeup

It takes a serious commitment to look like Gwen Stefani.

The No Doubt singer, 42, graces the cover of Harper’s Bazaar‘s September issue, where she opens up about her high-maintenance style. “I’ve always been a girl who loves to dress up. I already put my makeup on twice today: I put it on to take my kid to school, and then I went home, washed my face, and put it on again to have lunch with you,” she tells the writer.

Stefani even wears makeup at home because “I like to make my husband like me more,” she says of singer/songwriter Gavin Rossdale, 46. “And he likes it when I’m wearing makeup.”

Though she’s known for her six-pack abs, Stefani admits that she hates “talking about my body. It’s ridiculous. There is no secret: You just have to eat healthy, work out and torture yourself!”

Stefani hits the gym as much as possible, she adds, “but it’s more for my brain than it is for my body.” It doesn’t hurt, however, that constant exercise keeps her at a sample-size. “I like to wear clothes too much, so I try to keep focused,” Stefani explains.
