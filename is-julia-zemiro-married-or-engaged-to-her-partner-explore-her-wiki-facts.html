Julia Zemiro Wiki-Biography, Age, Height

Zemiro described how her parents first met saying,

Julia Zemiro went to Sydney Girls High School and studied for a Bachelor of Arts at the University of Sydney. She entered into the Victorian College of the Arts Drama School eventually moving to Melbourne in the year 1992.
