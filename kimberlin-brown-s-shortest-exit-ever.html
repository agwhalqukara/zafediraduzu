Kimberlin Browns Shortest Exit Ever

The Bold and the Beautiful comings and goings say Sheila Carter may be “dead,” but  is far from gone.  CBS soap fans will be seeing a whole lot of John Finnegan’s (Tanner Novlan) birth mom on the daytime drama this week.

Keep reading for all the details, here’s what’s coming up next on The Bold and the Beautiful this week.

According to B&B spoilers, Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) and her husband Finn will try to reconnect after she fatally stabbed his mother Sheila.

Sheila’s actual murder was bad enough, but the fact that Steffy told her husband she wanted his mother dead and gone from their lives right before she stabbed her is a tough pill to swallow.

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers – John Finn Finnegan Devastated Over Mom’s Demise

Finn is devastated over losing Sheila Carter, and his guilt is taking a toll on him.  Can he really be with someone who killed his own mother (regardless of whether or not it was justified)?  Not if Sheila’s ghost has anything to say about it!

Sheila Carter’s body is barely cold in the morgue, but Kimberlin Brown still has more scenes coming up as she “haunts”Finn.

Finn’s visions of his dead mother are making it impossible for him to forgive his wife or live in the house where his mother took her final breaths.

B&B Spoilers – Is Sheila Carter REALLY Dead

Ironically this isn’t the first time that Bold and the Beautiful fans have thought Sheila was dead and she came back to haunt Los Angeles, only for it to later be revealed that she is very much alive.

A popular theory on social media is that Sheila somehow managed to fake her death and will “haunt” Finn until he divorces her killer.  If that is her diabolical plan, it seems to be working so far.
