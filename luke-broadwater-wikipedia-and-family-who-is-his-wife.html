Luke Broadwater Wikipedia: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Luke Broadwater hails from Baltimore, United States. He was born in May 1980. As of now, Luke is 43 years of age.

Talking about his family, Luke was born the eldest of four sons of his parents, Daniel Broadwater and Katherine Broadwater. He has three younger brothers, including Andrew Broadwater and Alex Broadwater.

Luke’s father, Daniel, is a Presbyterian minister. Similarly, his mother, Katherine, is an art professor. Luke is of half-Arab (Lebanese/Syrian) and European (German/British) descent. With American nationality, he has mixed ethnicity.

Luke completed his high school education at McDonogh School in 1998. After that, he earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Writing at Ithaca College in 2002. At Ithaca, Luke was on the wrestling team and he also served as a collegiate wrestling coach for eight years.
