Priyanka Chopra Says She Cant Wait To Have A Baby With Hubby Nick Jonas Amid Pregnancy Rumo

The actress admitted that she wants a family with her hubby, Nick Jonas amid rumors that she is already expecting! Priyanka Chopra stopped by TODAY and while on the show, she gushed over her plans for the future with her life partner.

It’s very clear that Priyanka is super excited to have a child with the Jonas Brothers singer and she did not try to hide it in order to put the speculations to rest.

While chatting with host Hoda Kotb, the beautiful actress opened up about starting a family with her man.

She was on Today to promote her brand new movie, The Sky is Pink but she could not avoid getting a bit more personal amid the pregnancy rumors.

At some point, Hoda told her that having her two children was the best decision she could have made in life and Priyanka answered that: ‘I can’t wait. I really can’t wait. I mean there’s so much that happens in our lives that whenever God blesses us with that it’s something that we both definitely want.’

Even though there are unconfirmed rumors she is pregnant already, Chopra has never avoided talking about baby plans, making it very clear that she wants a child with her husband at some point.

She previously told Vogue India during an interview for the September issue that ‘Buying a home and having a baby are on my to-do list.’

The actress and the singer have been husband and wife since December and ever since then, they have appeared together at multiple events and on outings, including the Met Gala, showing off their happiness everywhere they go.
