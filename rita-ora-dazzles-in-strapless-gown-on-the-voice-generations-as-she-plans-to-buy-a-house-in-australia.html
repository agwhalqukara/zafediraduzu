Rita Ora dazzles in strapless gown on The Voice Generations as she plans to buy a house in Australia

Rita Ora has sent temperatures soaring as she shared a slew of snaps from behind the scenes of The Voice Generations in Australia on Monday.

The British singer, 31, looked stunning as she posed up a storm on the set of the new show, wearing a strapless green, black and white gown.

The midi dress showed of the songstress’ legs, while giving a peak at her ample cleavage.

Her blonde tresses were styled in loose waves, and she wore a pair of quirky heels with purple bows to elevate her height.

Accessorising her ensemble, she opted for a star theme, wearing a star-shaped necklace, earrings and clips in her hair.

She completed her look with a full face of glamorous makeup, including a dramatic winged eyeshadow and a classic red lip.

Rita posed for a snap in the iconic red chair, while she raised her arms in another shot showing off her impressive collection of tattoos.

She shared another hilarious photo of her fellow judges Keith Urban, Jessica Mauboy and Guy Sebastian pulling funny faces for the camera.

Captioning the pictures, Rita wrote: ‘And we’re back!! So thrilled to be in Australia on @thevoiceau Generations. The level of talent here is incredible, and I can’t wait for you to see it!! Swipe for a fun surprise from my fellow judges!’

The Voice Generations is a new version of The Voice, where contestants of all ages can perform together.

It spotlights undiscovered voices from each generation including families, friends, neighbours, student and teachers over three nights to reach the final.

It comes after Rita said that she is that in ‘love’ with Australia that she’s looking at setting down permanent roots.

She told The Daily Telegraph’s Confidential on Sunday that she’s setting her sights on buying a property in the Harbour City.  

‘I just love Australia, I have really found a great home here,’ Rita gushed about her latest stint in Sydney.

‘The weather, the beach, it is such a lovely community. I can walk to my local cafe, sit there and have breakfast, my local supermarket and butchers, I have just got my thing now, my places I go to.’

The Body on Me hit-maker went on to reveal: ‘I don’t want to speak too soon but I’m definitely looking into staying here longer and buying a property.’  

Rita arrived in Australia at the beginning of December alongside Taika Waititi, her boyfriend of almost a year.

Her plans on buying a property in Sydney comes after she revealed that freezing her eggs was the ‘best thing she ever did’ in a candid interview. 

The songstress told The Independent that she would ‘love to have a nice big family’, and also gushed over her romance with Taika, saying she ‘wasn’t expecting to find love’ with him.

Kosovo-born Rita froze her eggs at age 24 and then again at age 27 after seeking the advice of her doctor. 

She said of the process: ‘I’d love to have a nice big family! I think as women, we put that pressure on ourselves, subconsciously, because we feel like that is our duty – to create and give life.   

‘So, I just wanted to not worry about it. And I didn’t after, and it was the best thing I ever did.’ 

Elsewhere in her chat with the publication, she spoke about her romance with Taika, saying: ‘I didn’t expect to find love in Australia, nor was I looking for it.’

She added that she is keen to keep her relationship out of the spotlight and ‘protect it’, before saying she has changed the way she dates in the public eye now.   

‘I think protecting something that you really care about, you’ve got to have a sense of privacy a little bit. I wouldn’t have known that if I didn’t go through what I went through in the past,’ she said.  

When asked to elaborate, Rita said she used to be ‘so trusting and sometimes oversharing’. 

Rita met the Oscar-winning filmmaker in Australia in February last year while she was filming The Voice Australia. 

A source told The Sun the duo started ‘secretly’ dating in early March, three months before she left Australia for Los Angeles in early June.  

‘All their friends know about the relationship – they’re really into each other,’ the insider said at the time.

The couple made their red carpet debut at the LA premiere of The Suicide Squad on August 2.

Taika was previously married to film producer Chelsea Winstanley, but the pair are believed to have quietly split in 2018. 

They share two daughters, Te Hinekāhu and Matewa Kiritapu.
