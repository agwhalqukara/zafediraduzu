Who Did Demi Lovato Call the Most Inspiring Man in her Life?

So Demi Lovato recently started to come clean about what helped her through that very dark path she was on a little while back. While we know her friends and family were a big support, there was one person we didn’t know was helping to hold the wounded star up and keep her on her feet. A man she called the most inspiring man in her life. So who did Demi Lovato call the most inspiring man in her life? Keep reading to find out.

Believe it or not, it is Wilmer Valderrama. you know him best as Fez from That 70’s Show. Seems to the two have been on again and off again for quite some time now, though “on again” seems to be the order of the day. Demi Lovato tells MTV that he has helped her see what she is worth and has helped to keep her grounded during those hard times in her life when she felt like she was losing her way. Oh, Wilmer. We worry she may not know about your past. She may not know about all the girls you love and leave in Hollywood.

But who knows, maybe this is “the one” for both of them. Either way, if he is helping the young girl get through a tough time, we got nothing but respect for him.
