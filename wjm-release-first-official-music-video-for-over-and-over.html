WJM release first official music video for 'Over and Over'

Three-piece rock band WJM are back with a bang, as they’ve just dropped their first official music video for their latest track ‘Over and Over’.

We first introduced you to the trio, made up of William, Jeremy, and Max, earlier this year. However, since then WJM have released another EP called Animate, which includes the captivating ‘Over and Over’ in addition to five other tracks.

The music video for ‘Over and Over’ is a performance piece, with WJM delivering the song with great passion. Whilst the concept is simple, the video’s aesthetic is eye catching thanks to the use of colours, lights and effects.

Check out the music video for ‘Over and Over’ below.

‘Over and Over’ showcases a more mature sound from WJM, who are wise beyond their young years. In addition to being talented performers, the trio also have hearts of gold. Every dollar from their performances have gone to support children’s charities including UCSF Benioff Children’s Hospital, Second Harvest Food Bank, Camp Okizu, the Bridge School, Live in Peace, and St. Baldrick’s Foundation. Thanks to their philanthropic work, WJM were honored with the Novus Youth Social Activism Award at the United Nations headquarters in NYC last July.

WJM have had a brilliant year, and their success and great work looks set to continue well into 2018. We can’t wait to see what the future has in store for them.

‘Over and Over’, taken from Animate, is available now.

Make sure you keep up to date with the band on Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter and their website.
